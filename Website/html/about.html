    About Us

    We are building a website to predict the future price of crypto and the stock market. We will use crypto and stock API from CoinGeko, Yahoo Finance, and Polygon.io to gather our stock and crypto dataset. Our goal is to collect the top five crypto and stock; after collecting desired data, we will clean it using the Pandas library. We want to utilize machine learning to make future price predictions. We will analyze and visualize data to help users make decisions about purchasing cryptos and stocks.
